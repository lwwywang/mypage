  • Category: Company
  • Client: Customer
  • Project start date: 15 April, 2024

OOBE

This media player is intended to solve the problem that videos cannot be customized for playback in the self-developed operating system. Since the multimedia player in Google Chromium OS is closed source and only supports basic video playback and pause functions, the supplier hopes that we can provide a video software that at least supports video loop playback. On this basis, we have added some other functions of audio and video, such as fast forward/rewind, video switching, full-screen picture-in-picture, etc., and at the same time support user gesture operations and the summoning and hiding of gestures.
